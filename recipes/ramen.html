Ramen

Description

Quick and easy Japanese-style ramen that is a comfort food

The recipe is inspired by traditional Japanese ramen, and you'll love it because it has rich, savory broth and of course - all of the delicious toppings

Ingredients

  • Seasame Oil
  • Minced Garlic
  • Ginger Paste
  • Sliced Mushrooms
  • Chicken Stock
  • Water
  • Soy Sauce
  • Baby Bokchoy
  • Instant Ramen Noodles
  • Salt and Pepper

    • Steps

    1. Saute vegetables.
    2. Add liquids.
    3. Simmer.
    4. Cook bokchoy.
    5. Add noodles.
    6. Transfer to serving bowls.
    7. Add toppings.
